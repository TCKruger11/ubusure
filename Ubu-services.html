SERVICES

Through a highly professional and personalized approach, Ubusure stands as a reliable partner in catering to the diverse needs of both individuals and corporate clients across these various financial products and services.

Ubusure provides Medical Aid services, offering a range of healthcare plans and coverage options to individuals and corporate clients, ensuring access to quality medical treatment and services when needed. Ubusure's Gap Cover product fills the gap between medical expenses covered by traditional medical aid and the actual cost of medical treatments, ensuring that clients are protected from unexpected and potentially high medical bills. Ubusure's Life Insurance solutions provide financial protection to beneficiaries in the event of the policyholder's passing, offering peace of mind and financial security to individuals and their families. With Ubusure's Investment expertise, individuals and businesses can benefit from strategic financial guidance and investment opportunities, enabling them to grow and optimize their financial portfolios. For individuals and corporations planning their retirement, Ubusure offers specialized Retirement Planning services, guiding clients in creating personalized retirement strategies to secure a financially stable future. With Ubusure's Short-term Insurance, clients can safeguard their assets and possessions against unforeseen events, such as theft, accidents, or damage, ensuring quick recovery and minimal financial impact. Ubusure assists individuals and businesses with Estate Planning, ensuring that their assets are distributed according to their wishes, minimizing tax implications and simplifying the process of estate administration. For corporations, Ubusure designs and manages comprehensive Employee Benefits packages, attracting and retaining talented employees while providing them with valuable perks such as health coverage, retirement plans, and more. With Ubusure's Financial Planning services, clients receive expert guidance in managing their finances, budgeting, and setting clear financial goals to achieve long-term prosperity. Ubusure's Disability Cover offers financial support and security to individuals in the event of a disability, providing financial assistance and ensuring that clients can maintain their lifestyle and well-being. Ubusure's Dread Disease Cover provides financial protection in the unfortunate event of being diagnosed with a critical illness, offering financial relief during challenging times. Ubusure assists individuals in creating a legally binding Last Will and Testament, ensuring that their assets and belongings are distributed as per their wishes, providing peace of mind for themselves and their loved ones.
